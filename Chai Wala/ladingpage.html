About Chai Wala

Welcome to Chaiwala,the perfect blend of traditional Indian spices and modern flavors. Our story began with a passion for sharing the warmth and comfort of a perfectly brewed cup of chai, just like the ones our grandmothers used to make.

Today, we're dedicated to serving up more than just a drink - we're serving up a sense of community, a taste of nostalgia, and a dash of adventure in every cup. Come, sit back, and let us pour you a cup that will make you feel right at home.

Our Flavours

Black Tea

Tea which contains caffeine and polyphenol antioxidants , which give an energizing effect and offer health benefits It is the most oxidized tea, which gives a bold, robust flavor and is dark in color Black tea can be enjoyed with milk, sugar or even lemon

Butter Tea

Butter tea, is a traditional Tibetan drink, which is made combining black tea with yak butter and salt. It has rich creamy texture and savory taste and provides warmth and energy . This unique beverage is a cultural staple, offering a distinct flavor profile that is both comforting and nourishing

Tandoori Chai

Tandoori chai is a unique Indian tea made by boiling water, spices, tea powder, sugar, and milk, then pouring it into a heated clay pot to infuse a smoky flavor. This aromatic beverage offers a rich, creamy texture with hints of cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon, creating a delightful and authentic tea experience.
